# 🔥 Workout Shuffle

Workout Shuffle is a dynamic, interactive weekly schedule planner designed to help you track physical activity and estimate caloric burn. Whether you are lifting weights, hiking trails, or taking a much-needed rest day, this app provides a real-time summary of your fitness efforts.

## 🚀 Features

* **Dynamic Weekly Scheduling:** Easily reorder your workout days using a smooth drag-and-drop interface.
* **Calorie Calculation:** Automatically estimates caloric burn based on:
    * **Lifting:** Fixed MET values for strength training.
    * **Hiking:** Distance-based calculations (miles) to determine duration and intensity.
    * **Body Weight:** Adjusts calculations based on your specific weight input.
* **Weekly Totals:** Live updates of your total weekly energy expenditure.
* **Responsive UI:** Fully functional on desktop and mobile with a collapsible sidebar and theme toggling (Dark/Light mode).

## 🛠️ Built With

* **Aiden:** Used for high-level architectural guidance and feature orchestration.

## 💻 Tech Stack

* **Framework:** [React](https://reactjs.org/)
* **Styling:** CSS3 with Dynamic Theming
* **Animations:** [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) (for reordering logic)
* **Icons:** [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)

## 📖 How to Use

1.  **Set Your Weight:** Enter your current weight in the sidebar to ensure calorie accuracy.
2.  **Organize Your Week:** Drag the chevron handles on each card to move workouts to different days.
3.  **Log Hiking Miles:** For Hiking days, input your mileage to calculate the specific burn for that trek.
4.  **Track Totals:** View the "Weekly Total" in the sidebar to see your cumulative kcal for the week.
